Mercury

Mercury is the closest planet to the sun and the smallest planet in our solar system. It's named after the Roman messenger god Mercurius.

Mercury's surface is cratered and shows signs of volcanic and tectonic activity. It also takes 88 Earth days to orbit the sun.
